all about sour food

SF

Sour foods are foods that have a tangy, acidic, or tart taste. Some examples of sour foods include lemons, limes, grapefruits, vinegar, yogurt, and sour cream. Sour flavors can be refreshing and add a zing to many dishes. However, too much acidity can be overwhelming so use these flavors sparingly.
